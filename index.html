Shinchan: Shiro and the Coal Town

（Available on: Nintendo Switch and Windows PC）

Characters

Shinchan: The mischievous 5-year-old protagonist known for his hilarious antics and love of choco chips.

Misae (Mom): Shinchan's short-tempered but loving mother, always trying to keep him in line.

Hiroshi (Dad): The laid-back father who works at a company and enjoys his evening beer.

Himawari (Sister): The youngest of the Noharas. Loves shiny objects and brand-name goods. Smiles sideways, like Shinchan.

Shiro (Dog): The family dog — small, fluffy, and sometimes the most sensible member of the household.

Toru Kazama: The smart and responsible friend of Shinchan, often the voice of reason in the group.
